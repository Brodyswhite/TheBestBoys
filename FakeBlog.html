The Best Boys

Let me tell you about the two best boys you will ever meet. Winfield, pictured above to the right, and Jerico, to the left, are brothers as well as my babies. Jerico (Jeri) is Winfield’s big brother, and isn’t always as excited to play when his little brother tries to start with him. However they both love naps and giving kisses to each other and the rest of their family.

Winfield was adopted four years ago as just a 14 week old puppy. He had fleas and multiple infections but was ready to be someone, or everyones, new bestfriend. He has grown into a big, lovable, donkey of a dog who greats absolutely everyone at the front door with a toy to show to show he loves them already.

Jeri was rehomed by his mom almost 2 years ago at 9 years old. He’s a bit of an old man but I think he’s always been an old soul. He’s ready to hop into anyones lap and pur for scratches or find a new spot to nap in the house. Even though he’s not that excited to play he loves to spend time with people. This old man was a perfect addition to the family.
